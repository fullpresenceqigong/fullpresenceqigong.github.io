Please call, text or email to schedule an appointment or a class. 
Qigong classes: are form qigong classes for up to 20 participants. It will be a guided demonstration format of class. The verbal guidance will give queues to the movement and the breath. The focus of the class will change depending on the needs and wants of the participants.
One on one Qigong classes: will have the focus on the needs of the individual and will be crafted for their benefit.
Medical Qigong: will be with a fully clothed client laying down on a treatment table. Their responsibility will be to breath and relax while the practitioner works to align and harmonize their energies. The work will mostly be done by the practitioner and the client will be receiving.
Meditation classes will be seated and guided. Techniques will usually be explained as we are meditating. Sometimes there will be a description of the technique prior to beginning the practice.
