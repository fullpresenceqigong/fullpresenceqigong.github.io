In the Harmony of the Tao
     Qigong is one of the practices of Taoism. It is The process of learning how to be in harmony with the Tao. It is The study of how the Tao manifests in this reality. The Qigong student learns to decipher the energies of yin and yang and the energies of the 5 elements. How these energies manifest within the physical body, in our emotions and in our spirit. In what ways these energies flow, become blocked, and vary in condition. 
      Form Qigong is performing movements and series of movements that work with Qi. Qi is the Chinese word for energy. Qi has many qualities and subtleties. The forms are used to balance, harmonize and tonify Qi in and around the body.  
      A medical Qigong practitioner will work with the Qi of a client. Balancing, harmonizing and tonifying. This work also clears energies that are causing disturbances for the client.
